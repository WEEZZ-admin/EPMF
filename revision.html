Formula>0 / >1 means…

Quality & Process Capability

FormulaName
$UCL = \bar{X} + A\hat{\sigma}$, $LCL = \bar{X} - A\hat{\sigma}$Control Limits
$L(y) = k(y-m)^2$, $k = A_0/\Delta_0^2$Taguchi Loss Function
$Q = k[(\mu-m)^2 + \sigma^2]$Average Quality Loss (large n)
$Cp = \frac{USL-LSL}{6\sigma}$Process Capability (potential)
$Cpk = \min(\frac{USL-\mu}{3\sigma}, \frac{\mu-LSL}{3\sigma})$Process Capability (actual)

🚨 HIGH-FREQUENCY EXAM TRAPS（高频易错点）

  1. PM spends ~90% of time communicating
  2. PM does NOT authorize business case → Sponsor does
  3. Standards = optional; Regulations = mandatory
  4. WBS = Work Breakdown Structure (NOT Statement)
  5. Cost baseline includes contingency, NOT management reserve
  6. Cost baseline is output of Determine Budget
  7. Max cost at Executing, NOT Initiating
  8. CPI > 1 = under budget (good); SPI < 1 = behind schedule
  9. Collaborating = best conflict resolution
  10. SWOT used in Identify Risks
  11. Probability & Impact Matrix used in Qualitative Risk Analysis
  12. Quantitative analysis NOT required for every project
  13. After Identify Risks → next is Qualitative (NOT Quantitative!)
  14. DFM most effective at initial design stage
  15. 3 Pillars: Economic + Environmental + Social (NOT Technological!)
  16. 4Rs: Reduce, Reuse, Repair, Recycle (NOT Replace!)
  17. DMAIC: NOT Calibrate
  18. Pareto = 80/20 rule
  19. External failure costs = WORST type of quality cost
  20. Equipment upgrades = Prevention cost (NOT Appraisal)
  21. Taguchi types: NOT "Target-the-zero"
  22. BCR ≤ 1 = not worth it (unless intangible benefits exist)
  23. Opportunity cost can flip Make-or-Buy decision
  24. Strategy ≠ Vision / Mission / Goals / Plans
  25. Structure should NOT be static — Strategy-Structure Fit is dynamic
  26. Equivalent cash flows are equivalent at any common point in time — equivalence depends on interest rate
